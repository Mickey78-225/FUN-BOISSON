Our history


Fun Boissons is an expert company dedicated to the distribution and wholesale of spirits, soft drinks, beers and premium beverages. We are
a company belonging to the B&S group with more than 150 years of experience in the spirits sector.


We currently have more than 3,000 products in our portfolio and we do not stop expanding our catalog weekly with new references,
which places us as a reference company for our clients.


We strive daily to provide a personalized and quality service to all our clients, which, together with the quality of our products, makes
us the ideal supplier for your business.


At Fun Boissons we work with the main brands such as: Diageo, Bacardi-Martini, Pernod Ricard, LMVH Group, Beam Suntory, Campari,
Varma, Brown Forman, Osborne or González Byass as well as small independent brands that are beginning to stand out in the sector.
